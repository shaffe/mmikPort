Research platform overhaul

The project

Danske Bank decided to do a complete overhaul of an important client-facing service - a place where they share financial/market data. I was the sole responsible UX designer, serving as the meeting grounds between development and business goals. The project involved 12 business-side stakeholders, 3 developers, 1 User Experience specialist, across three offices in Denmark, Latvia and India.

If you want are curious about the live version, see it here.

My role:

  • Concept
  • Prototyping
  • Interaction Design
  • User testing
  • Quality assurance

Challenges

Financial reserach websites contain layers of pages and tens of thousands of article pieces. There's a need for a finely tuned navigation system that enables fast, keyword-based search/filtering while at the same time staying almost completely out of sight.


As the basis of the website overhaul, we had to use the existing research depository which was plagued both with indecipherable design and legal conundrums.
