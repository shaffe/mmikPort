Welcome!

My name is Marin Mikulic. I am a User Experience (UX) designer and Product Owner based in Sweden, with a solid technical background in system administration and front-end development. I work primarily with UX Design, and UX-oriented Product Ownership. That means I do everything to keep the end-user's needs at the heart of whatever the project is about. Otherwise, who's it being built for?

In day to day work I do everything from requirements analysis, user research, and roadmaps to continuous feedback loops, wireframing, and fast-iteration. I take care to learn from, and teach those around me.
